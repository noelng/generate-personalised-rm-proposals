## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Rimbunan Sawit Berhad (RSAWIT) is a Sarawak-based oil palm plantation company. Recent financial results show increased revenue, but an auditor's statement of material uncertainty regarding its ability to continue as a going concern raises significant red flags. While revenue has increased, free cash flow is negative, and debt levels are a concern. Given the current financial instability, a conservative approach is necessary. A Working Capital Loan might be considered with caution, but a Refinancing Facility is unlikely to be suitable at this time. An SME Loan could be explored, but only after thorough due diligence.

## COMPANY ANALYSIS (from web sources)

- **Recent developments and activities:**
    - Auditor issued an unmodified audit opinion with a statement of material uncertainty related to going concern for FY2024 [Web Source 1].
    - Termination of a joint venture to develop Sarawak NCR land, with reallocation of resources to core business [Web Source 19].
    - Submission of the annual report for the financial year ended 31 December 2024 [Web Source 9].
    - Appointment of a new independent and non-executive member of the Audit Committee [Web Source 6].
- **Financial health indicators:**

    - Net profit attributable to shareholders was RM2.75 million for FY25Q2, down 31.8% YoY [Web Source 16].
    - Free cash flow is negative [Web Source 11].

- **Growth trajectory and expansion plans:**

- **Specific financing needs identified:**
    - The termination of the joint venture was funded via internal funds [Web Source 19], suggesting a need for working capital management.
    - The "material uncertainty related to going concern" [Web Source 1] suggests a potential need for financing to stabilize operations.

## RECOMMENDED LOAN PRODUCTS

1.  **Working Capital Loan:**
    -   *Type:* Short-term loan to finance day-to-day operations.
    -   *Suitability:* Could help manage immediate cash flow needs, especially after funding the JV termination.
    -   *Key Features:* Flexible repayment terms, revolving credit option.

2.  **Refinancing Facility:**
    -   *Type:* Loan to replace existing debt with potentially better terms.
    -   *Suitability:* Potentially to restructure existing debt and improve cash flow.
    -   *Key Features:* Lower interest rates, extended repayment period.

3.  **SME Loan:**
    -   *Type:* Loan designed for small and medium-sized enterprises.
    -   *Suitability:* Could be used for various purposes, including expansion or operational improvements.
    -   *Key Features:* Competitive interest rates, flexible terms.

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Axiata Group Berhad is a leading telecommunications and digital conglomerate with a strong presence in Southeast and South Asia. Recent reports indicate a mixed financial performance, with revenue declines in some areas but overall profitability driven by strategic gains, cost management, and asset monetization. The company is focused on improving cash flow, enhancing shareholder returns, and reducing debt. Based on publicly available information, Axiata appears to be a strong candidate for a Working Capital Loan and a Refinancing Facility. However, internal verification of credit ratings and compliance with internal policies is necessary.

## COMPANY ANALYSIS (from web sources)

- **Recent developments and activities:** Axiata has been actively involved in strategic initiatives, including the merger of XL Axiata and Smartfren in Indonesia (XLSMART) [Web Source 5, 10, 14], and is focused on driving synergies and operational efficiencies [Web Source 3, 5]. They are also exploring the monetization of infrastructure assets like EDOTCO [Web Source 2, 17, 19, 25, 30]. Axiata has appointed new directors to strengthen finance, investment, and strategic competence [Web Source 2, 5]. They have also forged a strategic cybersecurity alliance with the Cambodian government [Web Source 5].
- **Financial health indicators:** Axiata reported a profit of RM431 million for 1H25 [Web Source 1], and RM160 million for Q1 2025 [Web Source 2]. FY2024 saw a profit of RM947 million [Web Source 6, 8, 11]. However, Q2 2025 showed a decline in revenue from continuing operations [Web Source 4]. The company has a solid EBITDA of RM11.1 billion in FY2024 [Web Source 3]. They are focused on improving cash flow and enhancing shareholder returns [Web Source 1]. Axiata aims to reduce its gearing ratio to below 2.5x net debt to EBITDA by the end of 2026 [Web Source 10].
- **Growth trajectory and expansion plans:** Axiata is focused on becoming a Converged Connectivity Leader in Southeast and South Asia [Web Source 1, 10]. They are driving profit growth in frontier markets and building value within their digital companies [Web Source 2]. The merger of XL Axiata and Smartfren is expected to strengthen their position in Indonesia [Web Source 10].
- **Specific financing needs identified:** Axiata is aiming to pare down debt and monetize assets [Web Source 19]. They are focused on improving cash flow and enhancing shareholder returns [Web Source 1]. This suggests a need for refinancing existing debt and optimizing working capital.
